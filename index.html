Why do you need a Specialist Cruise Cover?
Specialist cruise travel assistance and insurance offers cover for problems that could arise while you’re on a cruise - such as missed port departures or transport to a hospital if you fall ill while at sea.

All round protection - Provides cover not just on a cruise ship but for your entire trip

Cruise cancellation or curtailment - Covers cancellation costs if you or a family member suffers from unforeseen medical circumstances.

Missed departure - If you can’t make your departure due you’re covered financially.

Cruise interruption - Protects travel expenses incurred to reach the next port in order to re-join the cruise.

Cabin confinement - Covers losses if you’re confined to your cabin by the ship’s medical officer due to illness.

Unused excursions protection - This benefit pays out if you miss an excursion because of an accident, injury or illness.

Missed port cover - Protects you if a planned destination visit is cancelled because of bad weather or timetabling.

Emergency medical treatment costs - Includes emergency helicopter transfers, hospital and ambulance fees, and cover to get home if you can’t use your original ticket. And much more…

Frequently Asked Questions
Why should you buy Travel Insurance online?
Purchasing overseas travel insurance online is a convenient way to access a wide range of best plans at competitive prices from global insurers. This allows you to choose a suitable plan without incurring any extra commissions.
How much will overseas Travel Insurance cost?
The cost will depend on the kind of overseas Travel Medical Insurance policy you opt for, and it will vary from country to country. Multiple underlying health conditions will determine the final insurance premium you will have to pay.
Why do I need travel insurance?
Going on holiday can be a lot of fun, but a single misstep or mishap could completely ruin a perfectly good vacation. Your travel insurance policy will help protect you and provide you with assistance in case things don’t go as planned while you’re travelling. Right from reimbursing you for lost luggage to helping you pay for medical bills in a foreign country, a travel insurance policy will help you deal with all kinds of emergency situations.
What is the best travel insurance policy?
This depends entirely on your needs. If you only intend on taking one trip a year, you might find that a single-trip policy best suits your needs. On the other hand, if you travel incredibly often for work, you might find that a multi-trip or annual policy is the best travel insurance plan for you.
